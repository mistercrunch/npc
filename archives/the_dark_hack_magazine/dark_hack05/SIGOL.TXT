City info:
Sigol is a trading city, it has about 350 citizens.

Sigol is the main trading-point to the elves. You can always find adventuring groups in the taverns because of the good possibility of adventure in Konynk-forrest to the north and the Ornom-forrest to the south.

Sigol is famous for its supplyment of magical components (herbs) and elven magical items.

recent history:
Sigol was captured by the Hobgoblins a month ago and freed 2 weeks later. There are still serious damages, but only few inhabitants died and live comes back to normal.
