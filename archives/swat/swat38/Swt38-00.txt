bah, what a month. Ok, i have been busy as hell, and as a result, SWAT
mag has suffered!. BUT on the other hand, we have expanded our empire,
one step in many that we will be taking in the future.

So yeah, while i could have skipped this issue and put 100% into Krib
and my personal life, i decided "fuck it" and just put what i could into
it. 

Ok so whats the big thing about Krib? well those of you who don't know,
its the Warez site that is run as part of the SWAT Porject, it's web
master, is... myself :-p yeah i designed it, get it hosted, make it look
pretty and do general admin while working with all of my hard working
uploaders (before ANYONE asks, no i don't upload coz i only have a 33.6
modem).

Also MAJOR appology for not being able to get any warez disks to you,
i have no excuse, so just e-mail me and let me know what you want and
i'll do my best to get it to u.

And i'll wrap us this section by saying that hash in coffee is very good.
