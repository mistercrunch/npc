Put every thing in a shoe box, after making sure than no
bare wires are touching each other.turn the power to ON on
the car and put a lid on the shoe box. Goto the victims

the remote control and push on the button that usually
makes the motor work. if you put nails into the box, your
victim will be spending the next year picking nails out of
