Well the new year is now in, christmas has been and gone, SWAT moved hosts,
yeah, alls peaceful, or at least thats what we're told!

Anyhow, i'm not really drunk - given that i was almost poisons with bad
Guinness at a certain pub tonight, but all in all i'm peachy. Anyway, enough
of all the sentimental bullshit, i'm sure our hardened readers, or any
readers don't care to listen to me ramble on about the magazine that i love
and release each month (even if it is late!). Well, enjoy this issue and
congradulations to our CD winners this month.

Wewll i'd just like to add that i'm flattered that a dedicated SWAT
groupie took a lot of effort and put a lot of time in demonstraiting
his k-rad uber-design skillz on swats main pages, i'm impressed with
his 2 line message, but believe he could have put more effort into the
design of the defacement rather than the same kinda shit that u get on
"my first site defacement" pages, oh well, i guess that he was just
too jealous of us here at SWAT, getting known by our readers, making an
effort on the net and getting loads of hits to the site, hell, just
because we all get laid on a regular basis and all hes got is his
kid pr0n from DALnet, a tub of vasaline, an ovan ready chicken and his
right hand, thats no reason to vent his jealously upon the site. I'm
just impressed that we meant so much to him for him to put all that time
and effort in.

alot of memories... peace out.
