> fruibile da coloro che pensano che socialita', arte, musica e
> cultura non siano sinonimi di "business", ma bisogni di tutti,
> non solo di chi puo' permetterseli. Nel corso dell'ultimo
> decennio, nei locali di via S.Maria del Rosario, si sono svolte

> hanno partecipato migliaia di giovani ed organizzate
> sempre senza alcun tipo di finanziamento da parte
> dell'amministrazione comunale e senza fini di lucro. I locali
> stessi, ridotti ad immondezzaio negli anni dell'abbandono e

> parte, ristrutturati dagli "occupanti" (per il consigliere
> Consoli "fuorilegge"). La passata Amministrazione Comunale nel
> luglio '98, dopo alcuni mesi dall'ultimo sgombero, riconoscendo
> la validita' dell'esperienza del centro sociale Auro, decideva di
> sottoscrivere una convenzione che permettesse il proseguimento
> delle attivita' svolte, eccetto che per l'organizzazione di
> concerti, per i quali si sarebbe impegnata a fornire altri spazi.

> ristrutturazione. Questi nobili propositi sulla carta, non si
> sono mai, pero', realizzati.

> accordi,organizzando mostre, presentazioni di libri, attivando

> Around thirty city councilmens, guided by Marco Consoli (CCD), wants the
> communal Committee to commit an investigation about: " clarifying the
> crimes contested to the occupants of the " Centro Sociale Aurio", to
> motivate the break of the convention stipulated between the Last Communal
> Administration and the Association "Lorenzo Aiello"(ex Auro) for the
> allocation of the spaces in via S.Maria del Rosario (ndt. Catania -
> Italy)". With this demand, in short, seems that the communal council can
> emit sentences in place of the judges, since we know that no sentence has
> been emitted for these crimes (which crimes?) which motivates the break of
> the convention.
>
> The " crime " of occupation, contested however before the stipulation of
> the convention, and the consequent other " crimes ", have concurred to
> give back to the citizens a good they was prived, and to create a useful
> place for those who think that socialization, art, music and culture is
> not synonymous of " business ", but needs of all, not only of whom can
> afford them.  In the course of the last decade, in the premises of via
> S.Maria of the Rosary, numerous manifestations have been carried out
> (another " serious crime") to which have participated thousands of youngs,
> manifestations which always oranized without any type of financing from
> the communal administration and in a nonprofit way.  The same premises,
> reduced to garbage in the years of the abandonment and strongly degraded,
> have been cleaned up, rendered fit for use and, in part, restructured from
> the " occupants " (for councilman Consoli " outlaws ").  The last Communal
> Administration in July ' 98, after some months from the last evacuation,
> recognizing the valour of the experience of the social center Auro,
> decided to underwrite a convention that allowed the activities to keep on,
> except for the organization of concerts, for which it would have been
> engaged to supply other spaces. It promised also some indispensable jobs
> of restructure.  These noble intents on the paper, alltough were never
> realized.
>
> Although this the association Lorenzo Aiello has respected the agreements,
> organizing extensions, presentations of books, activating some services:
> one of them the " multiethnic ludoteca ", one of the few tries of real
> racial integration being very resolutive, and various laboratories
> specially the Medialab, currently one of the main activities of the Auro,
> that carries out course of " informatic alphabetization " putting to
> disposition a network of about fifteen computers connected to Internet for
> surfing, giving free email service and organizing courses promoting the
> use of Linux and the opensource philosophy. The all taking advantage
> mostly old computers, donated and recycled (ndt. from the garbage).  All
> that never demanding for financings.  The social center is also into a
> quarter " problematic " like "la Civita ", in wich the wave of human
> relations that got created thru the years between many various subjects,
> is a patrimony nobody can even think to give a price (but probably
> councilman Consoli would succeed in it). Sure an account, he, or who for
> him, has already made it and that is how much it could be earned from a
> beautiful speculation on the premises of the center, in order to pack
> there an agency for the temporary job allocation.
>
> The message of this majority is all in all very clear: we give a fuck
about
> the needs of thousands of youngs that in these nine years have animated
the
> centro sociale AURO, indeed we put to its place a structure that will
serve
> to better take advantage of the same joung ones in the sign of new
flexible
> Capitalism.  If then with this operation we close also one of the little
> voices of the dissent in Catania (the Auro always has been to the flank of
> the laborers, of the immigrates, of the homosexuals, of the
> antiprohibitionists in the their fights) the circle is closed.
>
> Nobody should be deceived that we will stay watching all this;  we care
> about this experience and we will defend it, also with the help of who
> express us solidarity sharing our dream for a open society in which rights
> and needs of people are respected. We invite therefore the Communal
> Administration not to give follow to an inopportune demand coming from
> novel Torquemadas that to the dawn of the third millenium still get
> prepared, in ours city, to burn witches. It would not be of good auspice.
